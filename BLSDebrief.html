The BLS Audit Debrief

Stop guessing about your career path. Use the Occupational Outlook Handbook (OOH) to get the facts on pay, growth, and what you actually need to get hired.

Open the OOH Website
1

Why do this? (Valuation vs. Intuition)

"Intuition" is choosing a job because it sounds cool. "Valuation" is choosing a job because you know it's growing and pays well. This audit proves you've done your homework.

  • Entry Requirements: Know exactly what degree or certificate you need before you apply.
  • Median Pay: Get a realistic starting point for salary negotiations.
2

The Magic Growth Number: 4%

4%
The Baseline

This is the average growth for ALL jobs in the US. If your job is at 4%, it's stable.

8%+
High Demand

If your job is 8% or higher, it is "Faster than Average." This means more jobs, more leverage, and more security for you.

3

How to Audit your Career

01

Search the OOH

Type your job title into the "Occupation Finder" on the BLS website.

02

Identify Gaps

Check the "How to Become One" tab. What skills are listed that you don't have yet? (Example: SQL, Project Management, or a specific Cert).

03

Bridge the Gap

Use LinkedIn Learning or university workshops to learn those specific "Hot Tech" skills mentioned in the OOH.

Final Takeaway

"An audit is not a summary; it's a strategic alignment. Use the data to make sure your career path is resilient for the long term."
